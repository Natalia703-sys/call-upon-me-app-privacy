Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Call Upon Me ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Call Upon Me mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

When you register to use Call Upon Me, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (as provided during registration)
  • Password (stored securely in encrypted form)

Users may submit prayer requests anonymously or using the name provided during registration.

b. Prayer Requests

  • Prayer requests are submitted voluntarily by users.
  • Requests may be anonymous or associated with a provided name.
  • Real names are not required.

c. Notifications

The App sends push notifications to remind users about prayer times and related activities. These notifications do not collect personal data.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Location data (GPS or approximate location)
  • Contacts or address book data
  • Camera or microphone data
  • Device files or media
  • Payment or financial information

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Allow users to submit prayer requests
  • Enable anonymous or named prayer sharing
  • Send prayer time reminders and notifications
  • Maintain app security and functionality

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Information may be disclosed only if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or security of users and the App.

Prayer requests shared within the App may be visible to other users, but personal identity is shown only if the user chooses to share their name.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your information, including secure authentication and encrypted password storage. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. User Choices and Control

  • Users may submit prayer requests anonymously
  • Users may update or delete their account information if supported in the App
  • Notifications can be disabled through device settings

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
